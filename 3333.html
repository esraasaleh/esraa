Details

Greek flag available to buy online at The Flag Shop. The Greece flag is available in size five feet by three feet, 152cm x 91cm, with two brass eyelets / grommets for hanging. Greek flags are printed with vivid colours onto quality polyester.
The Greek flag was adopted in 1822. The nine stripes are taken from the nine syllables in the battle cry for independence, which translates as, “freedom or death”. The cross on Greece’s flag represents the Greek religious faith.
